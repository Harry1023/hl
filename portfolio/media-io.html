Role & Constraints

Conducting User Interviews, collaborate with multinational team, analyze & innovate new features based on the reports. A Eight Weeks Project with tedious schedule as most of User Interviews were conducted during Late Shifts along with regular online meetings with other team members.



Focus Group Persona

With a User Base of 100K+ we had to segregate aggressively based on factors such as Geolocation, Interests, Hobbies, Backgrounds. The focused group was formed by picking individual from every major group. These major groups were individuals with similar interests determined based on Round A segregation. Each member of focus group represented a major group, each were given special names for further classification.


Competitive Analysis

To get a sense of the strengths and weaknesses of competition, I used & analyzed several online on the go Video Editing apps. We also had conducted a special interview series aimed at individuals that have background in Video Editing & Content Creation. The focus was to determine what matters for them.

This included giving them free speech on what features can be added to the app if technical & corporate limitations weren't present. This was a breakthrough as we came up with ideas that could've never been imagined with regular CX Reviews.

Some of the notable discoveries include:

  • Content Creators are frustrated that very few Video Editors allow directly importing Social Media clips to Workflow.
  • Video Editing products are slow and unpredictable. Saved files are usually wasted due to application crashes.
  • Due to poor infrastructure the applications usually aren't Cloud Compatible. The on the go web-based is just a nightmare.
  • Users hate the fact despite paying heavy bills no apps can handle real time rendering with GPU consuming tasks via weak devices.
